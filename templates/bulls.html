Bulls Bible School

Bulls Bible School exists to create an opportunity for in-depth theological training for people in ministry.

We aim at establishing students in their most holy faith, not just intellectually but with a keen focus on the spiritual aspect of our faith, with the intent of equipping them in the supernatural as well.

Why study with us?

Students come to Bull’s Bible School not to “stay” in their faith but to GROW in faith and to become the Spirit-empowered leaders they are called to be.

At Bull’s Bible School, we aim at equipping students intellectually as we couple it with the supernatural.

We produce balanced and holistic ministers of the gospel. Our training gives the learner the tools required to explore the depth of the Sacred Scriptures.
